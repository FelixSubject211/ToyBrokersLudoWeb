Regeln
Players take turns rolling a standard six-sided die
and moving their game pieces according to the number rolled.
If a 6 is rolled, the player can either move one of
their pieces already on the board or start a new piece
from the starting area. Game pieces can only enter the board
when a 6 is rolled. Game pieces must move the exact number
of spaces indicated by the die roll.
If a game piece lands on a space occupied by an opponent's piece,
the opponent's piece is sent back to its starting area.
Once a game piece has traveled around the board and reached the center,
it must move into its respective home column and be moved
into its home space with an exact die roll.
The first player to get all their pieces into their home wins the game.
